Backgrounds

What is a background?

A background represents your character's life before they were an adventurer.

Your 1st choice:

What is your background?

Why an Entertainer?

Someone who performs for others to gain coin and power.

You are good at physical activities based on speed or dexterity. You sing, dance, or play an instrument well. You can disguise yourself. You can play an extra instrument.

Your 2nd choice:

What is your background?

Why a Charlatan?

Someone who tricks others with various deceptions.

You are a good liar. You are good at concealing your physical deception. You can disguise yourself. You can forge documents.

Your 3rd choice:

What is your background?

Why an Urchin?

Someone who lived on the streets of a city.

You are good at concealing your physical deception. You are good at concealing your person from view. You can disguise yourself. You can pick locks.
