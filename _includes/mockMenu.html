Piña Colada

Potentially invented by bartender Ramón “Monchito” Marrero, 1954. The Pina Colada is a classic tiki drink, universally praised for its simplicity in taste and requirments to make. The drink traditionally consists of more than one type of rum, to give the apperance of being exotic (signature move of Don Beach). Here is my "what was in my fridge at the time" attempt to replicate this drink.

Cocktail

  • 1.00 oz. Light Rum
  • 2.25 oz. Pineapple Juice
  • 1.50 oz. Coconut Cream (Jigger Overflowing)
  • 0.50 oz. Orange Juice (Or other citrus such as lime/lemon juice)

Mocktail

  • 3.00 oz. Pineapple Juice
  • 1.50 oz. Coconut Cream (Jigger Overflowing)
  • 1.00 oz. Orange Juice (Or other citrus such as lime/lemon juice)
