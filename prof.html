What is a Proficiency?

Proficiencies are skills that your character has extra training or natural talent in. These are things that they are good at.

Here are your recommended Proficiencies:

Proficiency 1:

Explanation

You are good at physical activities based on speed or dexterity.

You work well with animals and they generally like you.

You are good at physical activities based on strength.

You know a lot about magical items and spells.

You are a good liar.

You know a lot about the past.

You easily know what others are thinking and feeling.

You can convince others to do what you want through hostile acts.

You notice small details when searching an area and can deduce their meaning.

You know about the body and how it can be cared for.

You know a lot about plants, animals, and the land.

You sense things in your environment quickly.

You sing, dance, or play an instrument well.

You can convince others to do what you want through logical or emotional arguments.

You know a lot about the gods and their teachings.

You are good at concealing your physical deception.

You are good at concealing your person from view.

You know how to navigate and survive in the wilderness.

Example

Jumping from one rooftop to another.

Riding a horse.

Identifying a magical scroll.

Swimming across a lake.

Presenting a guard with a fake name and identity.

Knowing who was king thousands of years ago..

Knowing if someone is lying.

Scaring off a group of bandits by threatening to beat them up.

Finding a hidden door.

Applying first aid.

Knowing if a plant is poisonous.

Spotting an enemy patrol.

Play the lute for an audience at a tavern.

Convincing your employer to give you more money.

Identifying a religious symbol.

Picking someone's pocket.

Hiding from a patrol of enemies.

Finding a forest path.
