What is a subclass?

A specialization that your character can take to make their abilites better for a certain role in the party.

Subtype Description

Bards that use new ways to inspire their allies. The can create objects out of nothing and animate the objects around them to help them with their tasks.

Bards that use their dazzling appearance to manipulate and charm others. They are masters of illusion.

Bards that use their knowledge of stories and myth to gain extra spells, skills, and tools for use in and out of combat.

Bards that perform in battle. They can use their music to allow themselves to special battle maneuvers of power and speed.

Bards that are also might warriors. They can use their music to inspire others and themselves to do more damage in battle.

Bards that use their knowledge and magic to uncover secrets and turn them against others through extortion and threats. Very street smart and politically minded with the ability to manipulate others in and out of combat.
