Why choose an archetype?

An archetype is a grouping of skills to cater a more balanced, effective character that fits your playstyle.

Archetype Description

Who is this Archetype for?

Creation is focused on being able to manipulate your surroundings and create new advantageous situations for your partygoers.

Creation is for those who like to say screw planning and go full hog the second a fight breaks out.

Galmour is focused on wowing and manipulating your adversaries (for pain AND pleasure!)

Glamour is for all the aspiring sociopaths that would love to take control of any and all individuals.

Lore are individuals who love to read and discover what the world means? Does this rock mean impending doom, who knows....or wait YOU KNOW!

Lore likes to find meaning in everything, kindof like your ex back in college who was waaaay too into crystals and horoscopes.
