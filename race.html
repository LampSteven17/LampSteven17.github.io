What is a race?

A race is a character's fantasy species or ancestry. It effects your chracter's natural-born talents.

2. Tiefling

Who are you?

Why to be a Tiefling?

Any person with an infernal bloodline. They often have horns, tails, wings, or other animal parts to show this lineage physically.

[More Explanation for why choosing this for this class.] You are especially smart and charismatic. You can see in the dark. You take less damage from various elements based on your demonic lineage.
