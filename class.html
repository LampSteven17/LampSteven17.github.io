What is a class?

Your details your character's abilities that they have and will gain. It shows what you can do in and out of combat to help your team.

Here are your recommended classes:

What is an Artificer?

Famous Artificer examples:

A warrior that combines magic and technology to support their teammates.

Iron Man
Dr. Heinz Doofenschmirtz
Kevin McCallister

What is a Barbarian?

Famous Barbarian examples:

The big guy. They use mostly use heavy weapons and do a lot of damage.

The Hulk
The Mountain

What is a Bard?

Famous Bard examples:

A performer. Supports their friends in combat and debuffs their foes. The most versatile class.

Alan-a-Dale
Orpheus
Jigglypuff
Star-Lord

What is a Cleric?

Famous Cleric examples:

A warrior of god. The best healer in the game, but also can do some big damage if needed.

Lucy Pevensie
Loki

What is a Druid?

Famous Druid examples:

A person who is one with nature. Can turn into various animals and use the forest to their advantage.

Poison Ivy
Groot

What is a Fighter?

Famous Fighter examples:

What the name says. Someone who is good at doing damage using any weapon they want in the game.

Captain America
Deadpool
Jon Snow

What is a Monk?

Famous Monk examples:

A warrior that uses their fists to do damage using martial arts.

Jackie Chan
Bruce Lee
Chirrut Îmwe

What is a Paladin?

Famous Paladin examples:

A warrior that works to embody an ideal. They pull strength from that oath to do magic and fight better.

King Arthur
Peter Pevensie

What is a Ranger?

Famous Ranger examples:

A warrior with knowledge of the land. They know the terrain and use it to their advantage.

Aaragorn
Katniss Everdeen
Hawkeye
Link

What is a Rogue?

Famous Rogue examples:

A warrior that hides in the shadows and attacks when the time is right. Often a little edgy, but especially dexerious.

Han Solo
Black Widow

What is a Sorcerer?

Famous Sorcerer examples:

A warrior that uses magic. Your magic abilities have been passed to you through your bloodline.

Harry Potter
Scarlet Witch

What is a Warlock?

Famous Warlock examples:

A warrior that uses magic. You made a deal with a powerful entity to have magic powers.

Dr. Faust
The Ice King
Green Lantern

What is a Wizard?

Famous Wizard examples:

A warrior that uses magic. You gain your magic through dedicated studies.

Merlin
Dr. Strange
Luke Skywalker
